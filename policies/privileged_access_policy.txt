Privileged Access Policy - XYZ Company (Finland)

1. All privileged role assignments must be approved via a formal change ticket.
2. Privileged assignments outside business hours (08:00-20:00 Finland time) require escalation to the Security Operations Center (SOC).
3. Privileged accounts must use multi-factor authentication (MFA) at all times.
4. Privileged access from outside Finland or the corporate VPN is prohibited unless pre-approved.
5. All privileged actions are logged and reviewed weekly by the security team.
