Incident Response Policy - XYZ Company (Finland)

1. All security incidents must be reported to the SOC within 15 minutes of detection.
2. The incident response team will triage, investigate, and contain incidents according to severity.
3. For incidents involving privileged accounts, immediate password reset and session termination are required.
4. All incident actions and findings must be documented in the incident management system.
5. Post-incident review is mandatory for all high-severity incidents.
